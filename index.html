About.

A short introduction

My background spans technical writing, instructional design, UX, training, and technical support, which means I’ve seen, from every angle, how people actually search for, interpret, and apply information. That perspective shapes everything I build.

I specialize in turning fragmented or fast-changing information into authoritative knowledge systems. By defining governance, information architecture, taxonomy, content lifecycle, and style standards, I help organizations create content that scales, stays accurate, and supports real work across teams.
